🎓 APC Waiver & Discount Policy

IJITRA is committed to ensuring that lack of funding never prevents publication of high-quality research. Waivers and discounts are available in the following cases:

  • Full Waiver (100%): Authors from UN-designated Least Developed Countries (LDCs) — waiver granted automatically upon request with institutional verification
  • 50% Discount: Authors from lower-middle income countries (as classified by World Bank) — subject to editorial discretion
  • Reviewer Discount (30%): Active IJITRA reviewers who have completed 3+ reviews in the past 12 months
  • Hardship Waiver: Early-career researchers (within 5 years of PhD) with no institutional funding — requires a brief statement and supervisor endorsement

Waiver requests must be submitted before or at the time of manuscript submission, not after acceptance. Include a brief justification and supporting documentation. All waiver decisions are made independently of the scientific review process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a submission fee? +
No. IJITRA does not charge any fee at the time of submission. Authors pay the APC only after their manuscript has been formally accepted for publication following peer review.
What happens if my paper is rejected after I pay the APC? +
The APC is charged only after acceptance. If a manuscript is rejected at any stage of peer review, no payment is collected. If a very rare case arises where a paper is retracted after payment, our editorial policy handles refunds on a case-by-case basis in accordance with COPE guidelines.
How do I pay the APC from outside India? +
International authors can pay via PayPal, international bank wire transfer (SWIFT), or credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard). Payment details including bank account information and PayPal link are provided on the acceptance invoice. All international payments are in USD.
Can my institution pay on my behalf? +
Yes. We can issue an invoice addressed to your institution or funding agency. Please inform our accounts team at accounts@ijitra.com at the time of acceptance and provide the institutional billing details.
Will I get a receipt and publication certificate? +
Yes. A formal receipt with transaction details is sent via email within 24 hours of payment confirmation. A digitally signed Publication Certificate in PDF format is issued upon final online publication of the article.
Is GST applicable for Indian authors? +
Yes. 18% GST (as per Indian tax law) is applicable on APCs paid by authors in India. The invoice includes GST breakdown. Institutions can claim input tax credit if registered. Our GSTIN is provided on the invoice.
