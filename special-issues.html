Special Issue on Generative AI in Education: Transforming Learning, Assessment, and Pedagogy
Guest Editors: Dr. Laura Martinez (MIT) · Prof. Sanjay Kapoor (BITS Pilani) · Dr. Yuki Chen (Tsinghua University)
📅 Submission Deadline: May 30, 2025
📖 Target Publication: Vol. 3, No. 3 (Jul 2025)
⏰ Calculating…
The rapid emergence of large language models (GPT-4, Gemini, Claude) and generative AI tools (DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Copilot) has created unprecedented opportunities and challenges for education at all levels. This special issue invites original research examining the pedagogical, ethical, technical, and policy dimensions of GenAI integration across K-12, higher education, and professional learning contexts.
