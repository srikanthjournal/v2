Author Guidelines

Complete instructions for preparing and submitting manuscripts to IJITRA.

📌 Before submitting, please read these guidelines carefully. Manuscripts not conforming to these requirements will be returned without review. For queries, contact editor@ijitra.com.
1. Types of Manuscripts Accepted +

2. Manuscript Structure & Formatting +

File Format: Submit in Microsoft Word (.docx) format only. LaTeX submissions are accepted with a compiled PDF.

Font & Spacing: Times New Roman 12pt, double-spaced, 2.54cm margins on all sides.

Page Numbering: All pages numbered consecutively including references and figures.

Required Sections (Research Articles):

  • Title (max 20 words), Author names & affiliations, Corresponding author email
  • Abstract (200–300 words), Keywords (5–8 terms)
  • Introduction, Literature Review / Related Work
  • Methodology / Proposed Approach
  • Results & Discussion
  • Conclusion & Future Work
  • Acknowledgements (if applicable)
  • Conflict of Interest Statement
  • References (IEEE or APA format)

3. Figures, Tables & Equations +

Figures: Minimum 300 DPI resolution. Accepted formats: EPS, TIFF, PNG, JPG. Figures must be numbered sequentially and accompanied by descriptive captions.

Tables: Numbered sequentially, captioned above the table. Avoid excessive use of color. Ensure all columns are clearly labeled with units where applicable.

Equations: Use MathType or the built-in equation editor. All variables must be defined upon first use. Number equations consecutively in parentheses, aligned to the right margin.

Color: Color figures are accepted in the online version at no extra charge. Ensure figures remain interpretable in grayscale for print.

4. Reference Style +

IJITRA uses the IEEE reference format. References should be numbered consecutively in order of first citation in the text. Use [1], [2], [1]–[3] for citations.

Journal Article:
A. Author, B. Author, and C. Author, "Title of article," Journal Name, vol. X, no. Y, pp. Z1–Z2, Year. DOI: XXXXXX

Conference Paper:
A. Author, "Title of paper," in Proc. Conference Name, City, Country, Year, pp. X–Y.

Book:
A. Author, Book Title, Xth ed. City, Country: Publisher, Year.

Website:
Author/Organization, "Page title," Website Name. [Online]. Available: URL. [Accessed: Day Month Year].

5. Plagiarism & Originality Policy +

All manuscripts are checked using iThenticate before entering peer review. The following similarity thresholds apply:

  • Overall similarity ≤ 15% — Acceptable for peer review
  • 15–25% — Author must provide explanation; may be returned for revision
  • Above 25% — Automatic rejection without review
  • No single source should contribute more than 5% similarity

Self-plagiarism (duplicate submission, redundant publication) is strictly prohibited. Authors must disclose any prior conference publications that overlap with the submitted work.

6. Ethical Declarations Required +

All manuscripts must include the following declarations:

  • Author Contributions: Using CRediT taxonomy (e.g., Conceptualization, Methodology, Software, Validation, etc.)
  • Conflict of Interest: Disclose any financial, personal, or institutional interests that could influence the work
  • Funding Statement: Acknowledge all funding sources
  • Data Availability: State where datasets used can be accessed
  • Ethics Approval: Required for studies involving human subjects or animals
  • Informed Consent: Required where applicable
