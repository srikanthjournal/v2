About IJITRA

Learn about our journal's mission, history, policies, and commitment to open science.

Journal Overview

The International Journal of Innovative Technology, Innovation and Research Applications (IJITRA) is a rigorously peer-reviewed, fully open-access, multidisciplinary journal established in 2022. Published quarterly by the IJITRA Publication Trust, New Delhi, India, the journal serves as a premier platform for researchers, engineers, and academicians worldwide to disseminate original findings across technology and engineering disciplines.

IJITRA is committed to the principles of open science, ensuring that all published articles are freely accessible to readers globally without any subscription fees. We follow the COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines for all aspects of journal management.

Aims & Scope

IJITRA invites original research that makes significant contributions to the body of knowledge in technology, engineering, and computing sciences. Open Access Policy

ℹ️ IJITRA is a fully open-access journal. There are no subscription fees or pay-per-view charges for any user or institution. All articles are published under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license.

Under CC BY 4.0, readers are free to share, copy, redistribute, adapt, and build upon the material for any purpose, even commercially, as long as appropriate credit is given to the original authors and source.

Article Processing Charges (APCs) are collected after acceptance to cover production costs. Waivers are available for researchers from least-developed countries. See our APC page for details.
