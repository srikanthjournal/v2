DOI & Permanent Identifiers

Every article published in IJITRA receives a unique Digital Object Identifier (DOI) registered through CrossRef. DOIs provide permanent links to articles regardless of URL changes. The DOI prefix for IJITRA is 10.12345/ijitra.

Articles are also assigned ORCID-linked author identifiers where provided. We strongly encourage all authors to register for a free ORCID iD at orcid.org.
